What is Elden Ring?

Elden ring is an open-world challenge RPG developed by FromSoftware, the same studio behind well-renowned games such as the Dark Souls franchise and the Armored Core games.
Elden Ring takes place in The Lands Between, a land which was once showered by the blessings of the Erdtree, a powerful and mystical tree which was given power through the Elden Ring. Queen Marika, the ruler who had previously brought prosperity to The Lands Between, shattered the Elden Ring, leaving the land a shell of its former self. The player awakens as a Tarnished, a people who were previously banished from The Lands Between, but have returned and are tasked with restoring the shattered Elden Ring.
Elden ring is known for challenging but engaging combat, deep lore, and for being open enough that you don't have to follow a linear path to progress the game, nor do you have to adhere to typical weapons or tactics, and has proven to be as engaging and even more replayable than any other game developed by FromSoftware.

What You Should Know

  • Elden ring is difficult, this game does not make you feel like a god
  • The Lore is all around you, pay close attention
  • Grace is your guide, be sure to follow
  • Sometimes the best choice is to come back when you're stronger
  • The world is completely open, but there are constantly signs trying to guide you in the right direction
  • Games are for fun, don't let other people tell you how to play them!
